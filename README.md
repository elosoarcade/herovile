# Herovile
**Herovile MCBE Add-On adds a powerful and unsettling entity to your worlds. It may simply be passing through — or it may attack you, dealing a massive amount of damage.**

Herovile is my own take on the Minecraft legend, Herobrine (created for licensing reasons). This entity will appear at night, anywhere in the Overworld. It is completely immune to damage, and its mood varies randomly — it may simply walk around the area, attack you in various ways, or even impair your vision for a short period of time.

- ⚔️ ATK: 3
- ❤️ HP: ∞

_This addon is optimized to run effectively on Realms and dedicated servers with minimal hardware resource consumption and its available in english and español._ 🇬🇧 🇲🇽
